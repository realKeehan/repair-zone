How it works

Two ways in: this website, or our Discord.

Send a request. Fill out the repair form here, or tap Request a Repair in our Discord to open a quick form.
We spin up a ticket. Your request lands in our queue and (on Discord) a private thread where we ping you and ask for photos.
Bring it by the booth. A volunteer claims your ticket, diagnoses the item, and gets to work.
Pick it up. We'll mark it repaired and let you know it's ready. Easy.
Heads up: back up your data before handing over any electronics. Repairs are done as-is with no warranty — see the Terms & Conditions.
